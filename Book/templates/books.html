    KAZI KUNGENXA KABANI NA

    The book is about the estate, the father of this family wants to share among his children everything equally when it comes to his will. Then, the first born refuses his fathers wishes saying that the estate is his hereditary rights. Eventually, the first born son kills his father, because he wanted everything to be given to him.
    To the Xhosas the general view is that the estate must be given to the first son.
    This book suggests that the estate must be shared equally, reason for that we must take one thing into account, a child is a gift from God. If we continue to share the estate unequally, there will always a be conflict.
