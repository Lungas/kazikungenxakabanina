    About the author



    Wiseman Gabavana is the son of Simon and Nowezile Gabavana. Born in the former Transkei homeland
    now known as the Eastern Cape at Tsomo in the rural village of Khuze at Xolobe.
    He started his primary schooling at Thembeni primary in Khuze village. He then moved to the Western Cape
    for his high schooling in Masiyile Senior Secondary.
    In 2003 he enrolled for LLB at the University of the Western Cape, but the following year he changed to Business Administration.
    In 2008 he graduated in Business Administration and the following year enrolled for the Higher Diploma in Education and completed it in 2010.
    His interests include writing and reading.His first book is "Kazi kungenxa kabani na" (Whose fault is it)was published in 2009.
